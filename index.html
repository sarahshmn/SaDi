SADI, two of us

I wanted to write you in Persian, but then I said I learned English from you so... let me tell you a story!
"On a day when she was bored to death, an angel appeared and took her hand. She felt a sense of familiarity, recognizing those soft and warm hands; she had seen him before.
She listened to his heavenly voice whispering, 'Don't worry! Everything will be alright. I believe in you!'
Overwhelmed by disbelief, she cried. She couldn't fathom the reality of an angel believing and caring for her. 'Is it real? Is there truly an angel who believes in me and cares for me?'
The angel guided her to another world, revealing its vastness. 'Look around, this world is immense! But fear not, I'm here. You won't lose your way. I'll guide you and stand by your side. Just keep going and give your best!'
Through tears, she protested, 'I can't! I'll lose my way, and you'll vanish! I don't want that!' The angel smiled and tenderly kissed her soul, helping her take the first step on that path...
Here lies a glimpse of what she learned in that world!
I've compiled our pictures to cherish those lovely moments and express gratitude for your presence in my life!
My angel, I love you, and I'm proud to be with you!"
